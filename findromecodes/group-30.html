What is Findorme ? FinDrome – The Finance Department Cell of NMIMS’s MPSTME Under the guidance of Prof. Geetha Iyer, Prof. Alaknanda Lonare, and Dr. Abhay Kumar, FinDrome aims to introduce students with the concepts and latest development in Financial Engineering and Financial Technologies Started in 2008-09 by two students of the class of 2010 of MBA Tech, Shahid Hussain and Anushree Goyal, under the guidance of Mr. P. K. Garg, the then Dean – Technology Management FinDrome organizes workshops, guest lecturers from industry experts, business quizzes, virtual stock market, management simulation games, case study competition, panel discussions, etc. in financial domain around the year
